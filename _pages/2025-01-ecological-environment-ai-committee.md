Ecological and environmental protection is a major issue closely related to the future development of humanity, and the rapid development of artificial intelligence is creating new opportunities for ecological and environmental protection and governance.

On January 13, 2025, at the inaugural meeting and academic forum of the Ecological Environment Artificial Intelligence Committee of the Chinese Society for Environmental Sciences, Prof. Ming Xu from the School of Environment, Tsinghua University announced a strategic collaboration with Beijing Baidu Netcom Science Technology Co., Ltd. The collaboration aims to combine Tsinghua University’s research foundation and accumulated knowledge in environmental science with Baidu’s strengths in artificial intelligence to jointly develop the world’s first pre-trained large model for the ecological and environmental vertical domain. This effort is expected to provide strong technical support for environmental protection and sustainable development.

At the meeting, the Ecological Environment Artificial Intelligence Committee of the Chinese Society for Environmental Sciences was officially established. Xia Zuyi, Secretary-General of the Society, expressed the hope that the committee would unite experts and industry partners, promote interdisciplinary technological innovation, focus on key issues in intelligent governance, and support the high-quality development of the Society. Representatives from Tsinghua University, the Chinese Research Academy of Environmental Sciences, the Information Center of the Ministry of Ecology and Environment, and the China National Environmental Monitoring Centre also delivered remarks, emphasizing the role of the committee in promoting industry–academia–research collaboration and advancing artificial intelligence applications in environmental monitoring, pollution prevention and control, ecological restoration, and ecological-environmental management.

After voting, Prof. Ming Xu, Chair Professor of Carbon Neutrality and Vice Dean of the School of Environment at Tsinghua University, was elected Chair of the committee and reported on the committee’s preparatory work and annual plan. The committee will prioritize the application of artificial intelligence in areas such as water resources and the atmospheric environment, and will provide training services for large-model applications to improve the technical capacity of researchers and practitioners in the ecological and environmental field.

PI Dr. Yuqiang Zhang attended the inaugural meeting and was elected as an Executive Committee Member.
