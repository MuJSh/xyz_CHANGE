Two of our group members, PI Dr. Yuqiang Zhang and Xuanying Ji, attended the 7th Ozone Conference held in Hefei, China, from April 11 to 13.

Dr. Zhang convened Session 4, **Extreme Climate and Extreme Pollution Events**, and gave a presentation in Session 5, **Theories and Technologies for Coordinated Control of Ozone and PM₂.₅ Pollution**, titled **Impacts of Marine Chlorine Emissions on Secondary Pollutants over the North China Plain**.

Xuanying Ji presented her research progress in the Young Scholars Session, with a talk titled **Changes in the Seasonal Distribution of Ozone in China from 2014 to 2024 and Their Driving Factors**.

In addition, Bin Luo and Xuanying Ji were recognized and commended at the Ozone Committee meeting for their volunteer contributions to the committee literature review effort.
