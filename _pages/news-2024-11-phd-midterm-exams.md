Congratulations to Zhuyi Wang and Bin Luo on passing their PhD mid-term examinations.

They are now officially PhD candidates.

Zhuyi's presentation was titled *Synergistic effects of global and Chinese climate change mitigation policies on future air quality and human health*.

Bin's presentation was titled *Modeling the impacts of emission changes on regional and global fine particulate matter and ozone pollution*.
