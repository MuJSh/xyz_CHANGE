Congratulations to Chengwen Niu for winning a Gold Award in the final round of the China International College Students’ Innovation Competition 2025, held from October 12 to 15.

**Project title:** SuperAML — Application of AI Distillation in Anti-Money Laundering Regulation

**Advisors:** Jianshuai Feng, Shanshan Xu, Ping Lin, Chaohui Yin, and Zhumin Chen

**Team members:** Yuhan Jiang, Haoxuan Guo, Shuyu Fan, Zhengyu Luo, Xiaocheng Liu, Yuqing Zhang, Wei Guo, Jianhe Chen, Qiuyu Feng, Ziming Huang, Chengwen Niu, Jieran Wang, Kejie Wang, and Hanzhuo Tian

**Team statement:**

SuperAML aims to develop an independent anti-money laundering regulatory system for China. Every step reflects the team’s persistent effort and the strong support from the university. We quietly safeguard financial security in the field of anti-money laundering, and grow through interdisciplinary integration into a Super team worthy of the times.
