## Research Areas

- Formation mechanisms and synergistic control of complex air pollution
- Construction of high-resolution emission inventories for air pollutants and greenhouse gases
- Assessment of environmental health and climate effects of air pollution
- Atmospheric numerical modeling and policy scenario analysis
