Climate change and air pollution are two major environmental challenges with substantial impacts on the planet and human society. Actions addressing one issue may have positive or negative effects on the other. The Institute for Carbon Neutrality and the School of Environment at Tsinghua University, together with *Nature Geoscience*, *Nature*, *Nature Climate Change*, *Nature Energy*, *Nature Cities*, *Nature Reviews Earth & Environment*, and *Communications Earth & Environment*, hosted the Nature Conference *Air Pollution and Climate Change* in Beijing from May 22 to 24, 2024.

The event provided a platform for natural and social scientists, as well as policymakers, to discuss the interactions between climate change and air pollution, the benefits of tackling them together, and actions for joint mitigation.

As part of the group, we had a wonderful time at the workshop and enjoyed the food in Beijing. Big thanks to the organizers at Tsinghua University and the Nature Portfolio journals for this excellent workshop.
